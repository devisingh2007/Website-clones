Places to Visit in Chennai

Chennai is known as the Gateway to Southern India and is one of India’s major metropolitan cities. It features beautiful temples, historic landmarks, beaches, and art centres. Major attractions include Fort St. George, Kapaleeswarar Temple, Parthasarathy Temple, St. Mary’s Church, Thousand Lights Mosque, and beaches of Chennai like Marina and Elliot’s Beach.

Other attractions include Guindy National Park, MGM Dizzee World, Vivekananda House, National Art Gallery, and Madras Crocodile Zoo. Shoppers can visit T. Nagar, Nungambakkam, Pondy Bazaar, and Parry’s Corner.
